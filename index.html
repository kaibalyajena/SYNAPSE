About Synapse . . .

Synapse is a two day fest at Bennett University on 18th and 20th April held by IEEE and IEEE-WIE promising you complete excitement, innovation and learning opportunities!!!

There will be three thrilling and technical events taking place CodeQuest - a coding challenge to put your programming skills to test, Bid-a-thon - an overnight hackathon for you to collaborate with like minded individuals to come up with innovative solutions and a Talk show "Beyond The Feed".

BID'N BUILD

Bid n Build is a unique and exciting coding event where you not only get to bid on different aspects of the project but also have the opportunity to build it from scratch by making the right choices.

CODEQUEST

Participants will be faced with various puzzles that they must answer in order to access specific sections of the program. Peice by peice, the participant must decipher the code and find out the goal / objective of the program.

BEYOND THE FEED

The talk show event you wouldn't want to miss.featuring one of the biggest names in the world of education influencers, the one and only Ashughai! as well as prize distribution of other two events.
